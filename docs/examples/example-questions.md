# Example Questions and Expected Outputs

Use these during development or demos to exercise different result types.

## KPI

- “How many patients were admitted last week?”
- “What is the total number of visits this month?”

Expected: single-number KPI card with the relevant count.

## Bar chart

- “Show the number of visits by department in the last 30 days.”
- “Compare admissions by department for the past quarter.”

Expected: bar chart with department on the x-axis and admission count on the y-axis.

## Line chart

- “Show a daily trend of visits over the last 60 days.”
- “Plot admissions by day for the past month.”

Expected: line chart with date on the x-axis and count on the y-axis.

## Table

- “List upcoming appointments with patient name and department.”
- “Show all visits to the ER in the last 7 days.”

Expected: table with multiple columns (e.g., patient name, department, scheduled/visit date, status).
